Earthling Sighting Log
Zephyrak

October 13, 2023 - Planet Jelloverse

It was a gloomy night, I was out in the middle of nowhere, gazing up at the stars and hoping to catch a glimpse of something otherworldly. Just when I was about to give up and declare it 'National Disappointment Day,' a blinding light engulfed me. My heart raced as I braced for an impending earthling abduction. But instead of little beings with ray guns, I found myself surrounded by a group of particularly determined fireflies, seemingly on a mission to confuse stargazers. I couldn't help but chuckle and think, "Well, I guess earthlings do come in all shapes and sizes!.

Xylophor

September 29, 2023 - Planet Fizzletopia

I'll never forget the night I saw an earthling - at least, that's what I thought it was. I was camping in the middle of nowhere, just me and the stars, when a blinding light pierced the sky. Heart pounding, I imagined I was about to become the protagonist of an epic sci-fi adventure. But as the light descended, it revealed not a spaceship but a campervan decked out in neon lights, blasting disco music. Turns out, I stumbled upon the party-loving aliens from the planet Groovion. They invited me in for intergalactic cocktails and a dance-off, and I quickly realized that while their spaceships might be rusty, their dance moves were out of this world!.

Zog

September 2, 2023 - Planet Quirkylon

I was staring up at the night sky, hoping to spot an earthling spacecraft when I saw a blinking light that seemed to be sending Morse code. I got excited, thinking it was a cosmic message, but then I realized it was just a helicopter advertising a local pizzeria with a malfunctioning light. So, it turns out the only thing out of this world was their pepperoni pizza.
